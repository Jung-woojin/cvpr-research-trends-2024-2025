# CVPR 2024-2025 Research Trends Analysis

> **Report Created:** March 2026  
> **Analysis Period:** CVPR 2024 & 2025  
> **Total Papers Analyzed:** 2,719 (2024) & 2,878 (2025) accepted papers

---

## 📊 Executive Summary

Computer Vision research has undergone significant transformation over 2024-2025.

### Key Statistics
- **CVPR 2024:** 11,532 submissions → 2,719 accepted (23.6%)
- **CVPR 2025:** 13,008 submissions → 2,878 accepted (22.1%)

---

## 🔍 Major Research Themes

### 1. 🧠 Vision-Language & Multimodal Models
**Evolution:** Foundation establishment → Agent integration

**2024:** InternVL, Florence-2, LISA, Alpha-CLIP
**2025:** Molmo, PixMo, Magma, FastVLM

### 2. 🎨 Image & Video Generation
**Trend:** Single-image → Temporal consistency, 3D, Multi-modal

**2024:** DragDiffusion, DemoFusion, ViewDiff
**2025:** DepthCrafter, SemanticDraw, MMAudio

### 3. 📐 3D Vision
**Paradigm:** Specialized → Unified foundation models

**2024:** SpatialTracker, OmniGlue, ViewDiff
**2025:** VGGT, UniK3D, FoundationStereo, MASt3R-SLAM

### 4. ⚡ Efficient Vision
**Focus:** Edge deployment, on-device

**2024:** EfficientSAM, MobileCLIP
**2025:** EdgeTAM, FastVLM

### 5. 🔍 Detection & Segmentation
**Revolution:** Open-vocabulary, zero-shot

**2024:** YOLO-World, DETRs Beat YOLOs
**2025:** Compositional Caching, Zero-Shot Anomaly Detection

### 6. 🤖 Visual Agents
**Breakthrough:** From analysis to action

**2025:** Magma, ShowUI, VADAR

### 7. 📊 Explainability
**Growing:** Trust, safety, regulation

**2024-2025:** Eyes Wide Shut, VPS, Gaze-LLE

---

## 🔥 Hot Keywords

**2024:** Diffusion, Open-Vocabulary, Foundation Models, SAM

**2025:** AI Agents, Zero-Shot, Temporal, Edge AI, Visual Reasoning

### Stabilizing:

### Declining:
